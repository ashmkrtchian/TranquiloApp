What is Tranquilo?

Mental health should not be a luxury.

Tranquilo is your personal space for relaxation, focus, and mental clarity. Designed to help you unwind in a fast-paced world, the app offers soothing sounds, guided breathing exercises, and calming experiences that reduce stress and improve your mood.

Whether you're trying to sleep better, stay focused while working, or simply take a mindful break — Tranquilo adapts to your needs. Build healthy habits, manage anxiety, and bring balance back into your daily routine.

Breathing Exercises

Guided breathing techniques that help you manage stress responses in real time, improve focus and mental clarity, and activate your body's natural calm reflex.

Guided Mindfulness Focus

Stress & Anxiety Education

Short podcasts, audio lessons on stress science, and bite-sized daily lessons you can engage with in just a few minutes — perfect for commutes or short breaks.

Sleep Mode & Mood Tracking

Relaxing sounds that fade naturally to ease your mind into sleep, plus a one-tap mood logger to track your emotional patterns and visualize your progress over time.

A classic cognitive training grid that improves speed and concentration. Numbers are shuffled each session to prevent memorization, training your attention and accuracy.
